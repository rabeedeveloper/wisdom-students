About Us

Wisdom Islamic Organization is an endeavour for spreading the message of Islam in its primordial form to maximum number of people worldwide; mobilizing and integrating the cultural, academic and intellectual resources supported by the scientific and technological expertise of the followers to facilitate the creation of thriving, dynamic, proactive and inclusive community.

  • Situates at Kerala
  • Created at April 16, 2014
  • Parent Organization is WISDOM ISLAMIC ORGANIZATION

More than just spreading the message of Islam; we also strive to uphold the human values by providing solution to the sufferings of the people through various charity projects such as Wisdom Relief, Wisdom Health Care, Education Scholarship Schemes, Self-Employment Support Schemes, Treat (a program for fighting the hunger), Widow and Orphan Care, Pain & Palliative Care, De-addiction Programs etc.

We are always committed to fight Communalism, extremism and terrorism; and will always be in the frontline for the struggle against alcohol, drug abuses, and all immoralities including sexual anarchy.
